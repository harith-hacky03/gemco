About

Everything you need to know about our Company

Our Profile.

GEMCO SCIENTIFIC COMPANY is a new generation start-up with a lot of professional experience. We incorporate all brands of lab chemicals, instruments, Glasswares, and apparatus.. GEMCO is mainly set up for providing new technology equipment & instruments from all over the world to each & every corner of our educational institutions, and industrial labs for all types of research and development purposes.

Our Vision.

To grow leading lab chemicals, equipment, instruments & Glasswares supplier in India.

What we Do.

We can provide a range of Lab Chemicals, Glasswares, Equipments, and Testing Instruments to all kinds of Educational Institutions, Research labs, Hospitals, Government Sectors, Bio Labs, Agro Farms, Diary Farms, Hatcheries, Poultry, Industries, Leather Industries, Rubber Industries, Research Scholars, Foundries, etc.
